Industrial farming has depleted soil microbiome diversity by 50-80%. Healthy soil contains 1 billion bacteria per gram. Depleted soil has 100 million. This reduces nutrient cycling, disease suppression, and crop resilience. Restoring microbiome with transplants is expensive and inconsistent.
