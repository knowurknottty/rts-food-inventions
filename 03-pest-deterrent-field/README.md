## The Problem

Pesticides cause 385 million cases of acute poisoning annually. They kill beneficial insects (pollinators, predators). Resistance develops in 3-5 years. Meanwhile, crop losses to pests are 20-40% globally. Organic pest control is expensive and labor-intensive.

## The Solution

Superconducting coils generate magnetic fields at frequencies that deter target pests (insects, nematodes, rodents). The field disrupts pest navigation and feeding without harming beneficial species.

**How it works:**
1. Coil array deployed around crop perimeter
2. Magnetic field generated at pest-specific frequency
3. Pests disoriented, avoid treated area
4. Beneficial insects unaffected (different sensitivity)
5. No chemicals released
6. Solar-powered, autonomous

**Components:**
- RTS coil array (perimeter deployment)
- Frequency generator (pest-specific)
- Solar panel + battery
- Pest monitoring sensors
- Control software

**Novel contribution:** Frequency-specific magnetic pest deterrence is demonstrated in lab settings. RTS enables continuous field deployment. No resistance development.
