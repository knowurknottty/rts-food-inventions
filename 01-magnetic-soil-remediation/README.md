## The Problem

10 million hectares of farmland are contaminated by heavy metals (lead, cadmium, arsenic, mercury). Crop yields drop 50-90% on contaminated soil. Remediation costs $50-200/ton using conventional methods (excavation, chemical treatment). In developing countries, contaminated land is simply abandoned.

## The Solution

Superconducting coils generate magnetic fields that attract paramagnetic heavy metals in soil. The field pulls contaminants to the surface where they're collected. No chemicals. No soil removal. Farmland remains productive during treatment.

**How it works:**
1. Coil array deployed over contaminated area
2. Strong magnetic field penetrates soil
3. Heavy metals (paramagnetic) migrate toward field source
4. Surface collection of concentrated contaminants
5. Soil tested and verified clean
6. Farmland restored to production

**Components:**
- RTS coil array (scalable)
- Soil preparation equipment (harrow)
- Collection system (surface scraper)
- Soil testing kit
- Solar power

**Novel contribution:** Magnetic remediation of soil is demonstrated at lab scale. RTS enables field-scale deployment. 10x cheaper than conventional methods.
