## The Problem

Seed germination rates are typically 60-80%. Poor germination wastes seed, labor, and land. In developing countries, seed quality is poor and germination rates can drop to 40%. Farmers plant 2-3x more seed than needed, wasting scarce resources.

## The Solution

Superconducting coils generate magnetic fields that prime seeds for optimal germination. The field activates seed enzymes, breaks dormancy, and synchronizes germination.

**How it works:**
1. Seeds placed in superconducting coil
2. Magnetic field applied for 1-24 hours
3. Seed enzymes activated
4. Dormancy broken
5. Germination rate increases to 95%
6. Seedlings emerge synchronized

**Components:**
- RTS coil (seed treatment chamber)
- Timer/controller
- Seed trays
- Solar power

**Novel contribution:** Magnetic seed priming is demonstrated in lab settings. RTS enables practical on-farm deployment. 35% improvement in germination rate.
