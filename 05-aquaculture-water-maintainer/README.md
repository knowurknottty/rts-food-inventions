## The Problem

Aquaculture produces 50% of global seafood. But water quality management is the biggest cost (60% of operating expenses). Bacterial infections cause 30-50% mortality in pond systems. Antibiotic use in aquaculture contributes to antimicrobial resistance.

## The Solution

Superconducting coils generate magnetic fields that suppress pathogenic bacteria while promoting beneficial bacteria. The field also enhances oxygen transfer and removes waste products.

**How it works:**
1. Superconducting coils installed in pond/aquarium
2. Magnetic field suppresses pathogens
3. Beneficial bacteria (nitrifiers) promoted
4. Oxygen transfer enhanced
5. Waste products (ammonia) decomposed faster
6. Water quality maintained without chemicals

**Components:**
- RTS coil array (pond installation)
